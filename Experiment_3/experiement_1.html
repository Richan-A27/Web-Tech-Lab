New Year Recipe!

A rich and moist chocolate cake filled with chocolate, perfect to ring in the New Year!

Chocolate Truffle Cake

Ingredients :

  • Cocoa Powder
  • Heavy Cream
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar Powder

Preparation :

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately.
  2. Combine and bake at 180°C for 30-35 mins.
  3. Cool and layer with chocolate syrup.

Easter Recipe!

Spiced sweet buns with a cross on top, traditionally eaten on Good Friday and Easter.

Hot Cross Buns

Ingredients :

  • All Purpose Flour
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Mixed Dried Fruits

Preparation :

  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately.
  2. Combine and bake at 180°C for 30-35 mins.
  3. Enjoy your tasty cross buns!

Christmas Recipe!

Rich plum cake with soaked dry fruits and warm spices, a must-have for Christmas celebrations!

Plum Cake

Ingredients :

  • Mixed Dry Fruits (soaked in rum if preferred)
  • Cinnamon and Nutmeg
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs

Preparation :

  1. Soak the dry fruits overnight.
  2. Prepare the batter with flour, sugar, eggs, and cinnamon.
  3. Mix the dry fruits and bake at 170°C for 40 minutes.

Diwali Recipe!

Sweet balls made of roasted gram flour and ghee – a classic Diwali treat!

Ladoo (Besan Ladoo)

Ingredients :

  • Besan (Gram flour)
  • Ghee
  • Powdered sugar
  • Cardamom powder

Preparation :

  1. Roast besan in ghee until golden.
  2. Cool slightly and add sugar and cardamom.
  3. Shape into laddoos while warm.
